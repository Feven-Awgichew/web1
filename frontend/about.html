About ASMIS

The African Social Media Influencers Summit (ASMIS) is the premier continental gathering celebrating digital creativity, fostering collaboration, and empowering voices that shape the narrative of a modern, vibrant Africa.

Our Mission

To unite Africa's most impactful content creators, digital strategists, and industry leaders. We aim to provide a collaborative platform that cultivates talent, shares best practices, and leverages the power of social media to drive positive socio-economic change across the continent.

Our Vision

We envision a globally connected Africa where native digital voices lead the global conversation. ASMIS strives to be the catalyst that transforms individual influence into collective power, reshaping global perceptions and opening new economic frontiers for African creators.

Why Attend?

Network & Connect

Build lasting relationships with top-tier influencers, forward-thinking brands, and industry executives from all 54 African nations.

Skill Elevation

Participate in masterclasses led by global tech partners (TikTok, Meta, X) focusing on algorithmic growth and monetization strategies.

Thought Leadership

Engage in critical panel discussions covering digital rights, authentic storytelling, and the future of the African creator economy.
